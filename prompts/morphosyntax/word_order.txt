We will consider the main order of the sentence in terms of Subject (S), Object
(O) and Verb (V). Options are as follows:

VSO: "drink John water"
SVO: "John drink water"
SOV: "John water drink"
VOS: "drink water John"
OVS: "water drink John"
OSV: "water John drink"

Next we consider the order of adjectives and nouns, which may be:

NA: "book red"
AN: "red book"

And adpositions and noun (phrases):

PN: "on table"
NP: "table on"
